Hello, I’m Piccia.

My home used to be in Italy, then in London, now in Spain.

Background

I’m a seasoned graphic designer and occasional photographer. I started learning about web design in 2013 and have never stopped since. I love WordPress and the community around it. I love to travel and aspire to be a digital nomad. Web design helps with that!

I am Italian by birth and nationality. I have spent the last 18 years in London where I saw and learnt more than I thought possible. Now I have moved to Spain where the living is much easier, and it's almost always summertime.

I am a die-hard believer in imagination and the need to keep it agile. I am always drawing or painting or taking photos or learning how to craft code.

I sell my photos, illustrations and time lapse videos online on the major stock photography libraries.

Goals

I've reached a good level of knowledge of web design and WordPress in the past few years. Now I am ready to become a proper web Ninja in all of the following areas:

  • HTML
  • CSS
  • JavaScript
  • PHP
  • WordPress

My highest aspiration is to be able to work while travelling the world. Besides designing websites, I am planning to produce and sell my own online courses and ebooks on graphic design.
