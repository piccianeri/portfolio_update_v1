Piccia's experience.

From art history to graphic design.

My story, in a nutshell

it all started with colour. Ever since I was little I was mesmerised by colour and the way it changes, and affects our moods. I decided to study history of art for this reason Then after university I set up a small arts publishing company with some friends, who introduced me to my first Mac: a Classic! with Quark Xpress! The first book I ever laid out was made on that piece of Apple history.

After I moved to London I started freelancing for design agencies with big clients, until I landed a job with the British Film Institute where I designed film posters and campaigns while directing the design department.

When I went solo again I decided to start learning web design properly, and fell in love with it. Especially WordPress. Now I mostly design and develop WordPress websites.

Besides running my design practice, I teach Photoshop, Photoshop for Graphic Design, Lightroom and arge for photographers at a great photography school in London: Photography Course London.

Skills

In the course of the past two decades I have honed my skills in a number of areas:

  • Branding and logo design
  • Publications of any sort
  • Adobe Creative Suite
  • Poster design
  • Creative photo manipulation
  • Travel, food and commercial photography
  • WordPress

I want to be able to work while travelling the world.
