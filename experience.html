Piccia's experience.

From art history to graphic design.

The beginnings

it all started with colour. Ever since I was little I was mesmerised by colour and the way it changes, and affects our moods. I decided to study history of art for this reason Then after university I set up a small arts publishing company with some friends, who introduced me to my first Mac: a Classic! with Quark Xpress! The first book I ever laid out was made on that piece of Apple history. Then a move to London, then more publishing, then pure graphic design, and now the web.
